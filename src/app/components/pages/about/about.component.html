Our Story

Founded by Yogesh in 2019, Sharma Vegies Place ☺ was born out of the need to provide a safe, convenient shopping experience during the COVID-19 pandemic. With the aim of reducing in-store rush, we introduced a platform where users could browse fresh vegetables online and pick up their items at the store, making it easy to avoid crowded spaces.

What started as a solution for safety has now become a preferred shopping method for many. The convenience of selecting produce from the comfort of home has quickly become a habit, embraced by the community for its ease and flexibility.

Why Choose Us?

  • Fresh vegetables straight from the farm
  • Flexible pickup times to suit your schedule
  • Convenient, hassle-free shopping experience

At Sharma Vegies Place ☺, we’re committed to bringing the freshest produce to your table with ease and simplicity.
